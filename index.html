About Me

Hi, I'm Cam!

An up and coming full stack developer with a passion for creating impactful, beautiful sites that have a positive influence on people's lives.

I graduated UC Davis in 2020 with a double major in Cinema and Digital Media and Gender and Women's Studies, beginning my pattern of hard work and dedication to my craft. I enrolled in App Academy a year later, where I developed skills as a full stack enginerr, and began my journey in coding. I'm mindful of design principles, and how the projects I'm involved with impact others. My goal is to create applications that can make people's lives easier, and provide entertainment, information, and services that positively affect people everyday.

Projects

WIP

Live Site GitHub

WIP (Works in Progress) is a blogging site deidcated to sharing creative ideas with the community. Users can add their works in progress, and keep track of their advancement through their projects. Community members can leave comments, share insights, or search for creative ideas for their next works in progress!

La Table

Live Site GitHub

Together with three other talented programmers, La Table was developed as an Open Table style clone, based around restaurants in the LA area. Restaurant owners can create their restaurant page, and add open reservation slots for customers to reserve. Users can search restaurants by name or cuisine type, and find information on restaurants' price point, location, reviews, and more!

unTokd

Live Site GitHub

The first site I ever made solo, unTokd is a fun cannabis review site, where users can rate the weed they smoke. There is much more to implement in the future, but for now, unTokd is a simple site for users to discover new strains they would like to try.

My Other Work

Games

Darkness Peering is a short, top-down horror game developed with Lua, by a four person team of beginning developers. Captured by an all-consuming monster, you must escape the maze-like hell dimension with only the help of the remnants of the souls who have been trapped there before. Escape the torment before you're captured by the monster, or die trying...
